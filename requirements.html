On-site Hosting Requirements

Venue Requirements

  • Sufficient space to accommodate expected number of participants (e.g., classrooms, lecture halls, common areas).
  • Reliable high-speed internet access (Wi-Fi) for all participants.
  • Adequate power outlets for charging devices.
  • Projectors or large screens for presentations and announcements.
  • Whiteboards or flip charts for brainstorming sessions.
  • Restroom facilities.
  • Accessibility for individuals with disabilities.

Technical Infrastructure

  • Dedicated network support staff on-site during the event.
  • Backup internet connection (if possible).
  • Basic audio-visual equipment (microphones, speakers).
  • Technical support for participants (e.g., troubleshooting network issues).

Logistical Support

  • On-site registration and check-in area.
  • Designated areas for team collaboration and quiet work.
  • Access to food and beverage options (e.g., nearby cafes, catering options).
  • First aid station or access to medical assistance.
  • Clear signage for navigation within the venue.
  • Security personnel (if required by venue or event size).

Promotional & Communication

  • Ability to promote the event locally within your institution/community.
  • Designated point of contact for coordination with the global hackathon organizers.
  • Willingness to share local event photos and highlights for global promotion.
